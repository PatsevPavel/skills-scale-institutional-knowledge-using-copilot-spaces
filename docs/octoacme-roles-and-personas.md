# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile practices and coach teams on process improvements. They remove blockers, enable self-organization, and help teams deliver value incrementally.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Coach the team on Scrum practices and continuous improvement
- Identify and help remove team blockers and impediments
- Work closely with Project Managers and Product Managers to ensure smooth workflow
- Shield the team from external distractions and maintain focus on sprint goals
- Track team velocity and help optimize delivery cadence

### Goals
- Enable high-performing, self-organizing teams
- Improve team predictability and delivery rhythm
- Foster a culture of continuous learning and improvement

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of Agile ceremonies
- One-on-ones with team members
- Escalation of blockers to Project Manager
- Metrics and velocity reports to PM and Product Manager

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Project Manager** on timeline and risk management
- Collaborates with **Product Manager** on backlog refinement and prioritization
- Supports **Developers** by removing impediments and facilitating collaboration

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user research, design, and validation to ensure solutions are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs. They are advocates for the user perspective throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Collaborate with Product Managers to define user requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design feasibility and pixel-perfect implementation
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and metrics
- Document design systems and design decisions

### Goals
- Create delightful, user-centric experiences
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques
- User research findings and reports
- Collaboration in planning meetings to discuss usability implications
- Design specifications and documentation

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** on problem definition and success metrics
- Collaborates with **Developers** on implementation and design-to-code handoff
- Gathers feedback from **Stakeholders** on user experience
- Works with **QA/Testing** on acceptance criteria related to UX

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts measure product and project outcomes, interpret metrics, and provide insights to inform decision-making. They help teams understand the impact of their work and optimize based on data.

### Responsibilities
- Define, track, and report on success metrics and KPIs
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Conduct A/B tests and measure experiments
- Provide insights and recommendations to Product Managers and leadership
- Build dashboards and monitoring tools for key signals
- Identify trends and opportunities for optimization

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making
- Quantify the impact of delivered features
- Identify optimization opportunities and areas for improvement

### Typical Communication
- Metrics dashboards and weekly reports
- Data-driven recommendations to Product Manager
- Insights presentations to stakeholders
- Collaboration on defining success criteria during planning

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** on metric definition and prioritization
- Works with **Developers** to instrument code and collect data
- Provides insights to **Project Managers** on project health and outcomes
- Reports to **Stakeholders** on business impact and ROI

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers orchestrate the release and deployment process, ensuring smooth, auditable, and low-risk transitions to production. They coordinate across teams and manage the release timeline.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule release windows
- Coordinate with Developers, QA, and Operations on deployment readiness
- Manage release notes and communication to stakeholders
- Execute or oversee deployment to staging and production
- Monitor post-deployment health and coordinate rollback if needed
- Maintain release calendars, versioning, and deployment logs
- Coordinate incident response if critical issues arise post-release

### Goals
- Minimize deployment risk and downtime
- Ensure consistent, auditable release processes
- Maintain clear communication with all stakeholders during releases

### Typical Communication
- Release plans and deployment schedules
- Pre-release readiness checklists and sign-offs
- Release notes and deployment status updates
- Post-deployment monitoring and incident reports

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works with **Developers** on code readiness and deployment coordination
- Collaborates with **QA/Testing** on release validation and smoke tests
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** on timeline and stakeholder communication
- Reports to **Stakeholders** on release status and business impact

---

## Stakeholder / Business Owner

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Business Owners define business goals, provide strategic direction, and ensure projects align with business objectives. They are decision-makers and champions for the work.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria
- Provide funding, resources, and strategic direction
- Participate in project kickoffs, reviews, and major decisions
- Monitor progress and escalate business-impacting issues
- Communicate outcomes and ROI to leadership
- Provide feedback on deliverables and user impact

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Maintain alignment between product delivery and business strategy
- Maximize ROI and stakeholder satisfaction

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and reviews
- Executive summaries and business impact reports
- Decision meetings and steering committee interactions
- Feedback on delivered features and outcomes

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Project Managers** on timeline, scope, and risk escalation
- Works with **Product Managers** on strategic priorities and success metrics
- Receives insights from **Data Analysts** on business impact and ROI
- Provides feedback to **Developers** and **UX Designers** on business requirements
